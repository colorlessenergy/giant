Who is the Giant

Most efficient Tank in Clash Royale and Clash of Clans

Giants prioritize defensive structures above all other targets, and will bypass all other types of enemy buildings and troops while any defenses remain on the battlefield.

The Giant is unlocked in the beginning in the training camp. He is a building-targeting, the giant is a melee troop with high hitpoints and decent damage. He wears a large, brown, coat and has ginger sideburns and blue eyes.
